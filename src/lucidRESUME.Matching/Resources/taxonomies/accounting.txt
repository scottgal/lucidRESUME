# Accounting skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

general ledger: gl, journal entries, chart of accounts
accounts payable: ap, vendor management, invoice processing, purchase orders
accounts receivable: ar, billing, collections, invoicing, credit control
bank reconciliation: account reconciliation, statement reconciliation
payroll: payroll processing, payroll taxes, wage calculation
tax preparation: tax filing, tax returns, income tax, corporate tax, vat, gst
audit: internal audit, external audit, audit preparation, compliance audit
budgeting: budget preparation, variance analysis, cost control
financial statements: balance sheet, income statement, profit and loss, p&l, cash flow statement
cost accounting: cost analysis, job costing, standard costing, activity-based costing
gaap: generally accepted accounting principles, accounting standards
ifrs: international financial reporting standards
sox: sarbanes-oxley, sox compliance, internal controls
quickbooks: intuit quickbooks, qbo, quickbooks online
sage: sage 50, sage 100, sage intacct
sap: sap fico, sap erp, sap business one
xero: cloud accounting
excel: spreadsheets, pivot tables, vlookup, formulas
erp: enterprise resource planning, oracle, netsuite, dynamics
cpa: certified public accountant
cma: certified management accountant
bookkeeping: double-entry bookkeeping, trial balance
depreciation: fixed assets, asset management, amortization
