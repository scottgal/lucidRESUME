# Banking skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

retail banking: consumer banking, personal banking, branch banking
commercial banking: business banking, corporate banking, lending
credit analysis: credit assessment, creditworthiness, risk rating
loan origination: mortgage, personal loans, auto loans, loan processing
underwriting: credit underwriting, risk underwriting, loan approval
kyc: know your customer, customer due diligence, cdd, identity verification
aml: anti-money laundering, bsa, bank secrecy act, suspicious activity, sar
regulatory compliance: occ, fdic, federal reserve, cfpb, dodd-frank
deposit operations: account opening, cash management, wire transfers
treasury: cash flow, liquidity management, foreign exchange, fx
wealth management: private banking, portfolio management, financial advisory
investment: securities, equities, fixed income, mutual funds, etf
risk management: credit risk, market risk, operational risk, enterprise risk
relationship management: client management, customer retention, cross-selling
teller operations: cash handling, transactions, customer service, vault
online banking: digital banking, mobile banking, internet banking, fintech
payment processing: ach, swift, wire transfer, rtgs, card processing
fraud detection: fraud prevention, transaction monitoring, chargeback
core banking: fiserv, jack henry, temenos, finacle, banking software
series 6: series 7, series 63, series 65, finra, securities license
