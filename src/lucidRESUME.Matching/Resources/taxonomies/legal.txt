# Legal / Advocate skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

litigation: civil litigation, trial, courtroom, dispute resolution
legal research: westlaw, lexisnexis, case law, statutory research
contract law: contract drafting, contract review, agreement, terms and conditions
corporate law: business law, mergers, acquisitions, incorporation
family law: divorce, custody, child support, family court, domestic relations
criminal law: defense, prosecution, criminal justice, penal code
immigration law: visa, green card, deportation, asylum, work permit
intellectual property: ip, patent, trademark, copyright, trade secret
real estate law: property law, conveyancing, title search, closing
employment law: labor law, discrimination, wrongful termination, eeoc, ada
compliance: regulatory compliance, gdpr, hipaa, anti-corruption, ethics
legal writing: briefs, memoranda, motions, pleadings, opinions
client counseling: legal advice, client consultation, case strategy
mediation: arbitration, adr, alternative dispute resolution, negotiation
due diligence: investigation, background research, risk assessment
paralegal: legal assistant, case management, document review
case management: docket management, deadline tracking, filing
notary: notarization, apostille, document authentication
pro bono: public interest, legal aid, community service
bar admission: bar exam, licensed attorney, jd, law degree
