# Finance skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

# Core Finance
financial analysis: financial modeling, financial planning, fp&a
budgeting: budget management, budget forecasting, cost analysis
forecasting: financial forecasting, revenue projection, trend analysis
gaap: generally accepted accounting principles, us gaap, ifrs
audit: internal audit, external audit, sox compliance, sarbanes-oxley
tax: tax preparation, tax planning, corporate tax, income tax, vat
bookkeeping: general ledger, journal entries, double-entry
accounts payable: ap, vendor payments, invoice processing
accounts receivable: ar, collections, billing, invoicing
reconciliation: bank reconciliation, account reconciliation
payroll: payroll processing, compensation, benefits administration
cash flow: cash management, treasury, liquidity management
financial reporting: management reporting, quarterly reports, annual reports

# Banking & Investment
investment banking: m&a, mergers and acquisitions, deal structuring
portfolio management: asset management, wealth management, fund management
risk management: credit risk, market risk, operational risk, var
compliance: regulatory compliance, aml, anti-money laundering, kyc, know your customer
trading: equity trading, fixed income, derivatives, options, futures
underwriting: credit underwriting, insurance underwriting, loan origination
mortgage: mortgage lending, loan servicing, real estate finance
private equity: venture capital, pe, vc, lbo, leveraged buyout

# Tools & Systems
excel: spreadsheets, vlookup, pivot tables, vba, macros
sap: erp, enterprise resource planning, sap fico, sap hana
quickbooks: accounting software, intuit
oracle financials: oracle erp, peoplesoft
bloomberg: bloomberg terminal, market data
tableau: data visualization, business intelligence, power bi
sql: database queries, data extraction
python: financial modeling, quantitative analysis

# Certifications & Standards
cpa: certified public accountant
cfa: chartered financial analyst
cfp: certified financial planner
acca: association of chartered certified accountants
series 7: securities license, finra
series 63: state securities license
cma: certified management accountant
