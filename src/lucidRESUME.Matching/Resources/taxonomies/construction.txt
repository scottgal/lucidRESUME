# Construction skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

project management: construction management, site management, general contracting
estimating: cost estimating, quantity surveying, takeoff, bid preparation
scheduling: project scheduling, critical path, cpm, gantt, primavera, ms project
blueprint reading: plan reading, construction drawings, architectural plans, shop drawings
safety: osha, site safety, safety management, toolbox talks, jsa, ppe
quality control: qc, inspection, punch list, quality assurance, qa
concrete: concrete placement, formwork, rebar, finishing, flatwork
framing: wood framing, steel framing, structural framing, carpentry
electrical: electrical installation, wiring, conduit, panel, nec
plumbing: pipe fitting, drainage, water supply, sewage
hvac: heating, ventilation, air conditioning, ductwork, mechanical
roofing: roof installation, waterproofing, membrane, shingles
heavy equipment: crane operation, excavator, bulldozer, forklift, backhoe
surveying: land surveying, layout, grading, gps, total station
welding: mig, tig, stick welding, fabrication, structural welding
building codes: ibc, local codes, permits, inspections, code compliance
leed: green building, sustainable construction, energy efficiency
bim: building information modeling, revit, navisworks
subcontractor management: trade coordination, vendor management
change orders: contract modifications, scope changes, claims
