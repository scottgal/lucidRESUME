# Healthcare skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

# Clinical
patient care: bedside care, direct patient care, patient assessment
vital signs: blood pressure, heart rate, temperature monitoring
medication administration: drug dispensing, pharmaceutical administration, med pass
wound care: dressing changes, wound management, debridement
iv therapy: intravenous therapy, iv insertion, infusion therapy
phlebotomy: blood draw, venipuncture
cpr: cardiopulmonary resuscitation, bls, basic life support, acls, advanced cardiac life support
triage: patient prioritization, emergency assessment
ehr: electronic health records, emr, electronic medical records, epic, cerner, meditech
hipaa: health information privacy, patient confidentiality, phi
icd-10: medical coding, diagnostic coding, cpt coding
medical terminology: clinical terminology, anatomy
infection control: sterile technique, aseptic technique, hand hygiene

# Nursing
registered nurse: rn, bsn, nursing
licensed practical nurse: lpn, lvn
nurse practitioner: np, arnp, aprn
charge nurse: nursing supervisor, shift lead
care planning: nursing care plan, patient care plan
patient education: health education, discharge planning

# Allied Health
physical therapy: pt, physiotherapy, rehabilitation
occupational therapy: ot, functional rehabilitation
speech therapy: slp, speech-language pathology
radiology: x-ray, imaging, ct scan, mri, ultrasound
laboratory: lab technician, clinical laboratory, pathology
pharmacy: pharmacist, medication management, drug interaction
respiratory therapy: rt, ventilator management, pulmonary function

# Administration
healthcare administration: hospital administration, clinic management
medical billing: claims processing, revenue cycle, coding and billing
insurance verification: prior authorization, benefits verification
patient scheduling: appointment management
medical records: health information management, him, chart management
quality improvement: qi, quality assurance, performance improvement
joint commission: jcaho, accreditation, regulatory compliance

# Specialties
oncology: cancer care, chemotherapy, radiation therapy
cardiology: cardiac care, ecg, electrocardiogram, heart catheterization
orthopedics: musculoskeletal, joint replacement, fracture care
pediatrics: child healthcare, neonatal, nicu
geriatrics: elder care, long-term care, assisted living
mental health: psychiatric nursing, behavioral health, counseling
emergency medicine: er, emergency department, trauma
surgery: operating room, or, perioperative, surgical tech
icu: intensive care unit, critical care
