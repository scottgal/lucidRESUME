# Aviation skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

aircraft maintenance: airframe, powerplant, a&p, aircraft repair
avionics: aircraft electronics, flight instruments, navigation systems, autopilot
faa regulations: far, federal aviation regulations, part 121, part 135, part 91
safety management: sms, safety management system, risk assessment
flight operations: flight planning, dispatch, crew scheduling
pilot: commercial pilot, atp, airline transport pilot, cpl, ppl
air traffic control: atc, radar, approach control, tower
ground handling: ramp operations, baggage handling, aircraft marshalling
mro: maintenance repair overhaul, heavy maintenance, line maintenance
ndt: non-destructive testing, ultrasonic, eddy current, magnetic particle
composite repair: composite structures, fiberglass, carbon fiber
sheet metal: aircraft skin repair, riveting, structural repair
hydraulics: aircraft hydraulic systems, landing gear, flight controls
pneumatics: bleed air, pressurization, environmental control
turbine engine: jet engine, cfm56, pw4000, trent, turbofan
reciprocating engine: piston engine, lycoming, continental
eia: electrical interconnect assembly, wire harness, avionics installation
boeing: 737, 747, 777, 787, boeing aircraft
airbus: a320, a330, a350, a380, airbus aircraft
