# Engineering skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

# Mechanical
cad: computer-aided design, autocad, solidworks, catia, creo, inventor, fusion 360
cfd: computational fluid dynamics, ansys fluent, openfoam
fea: finite element analysis, ansys, abaqus, nastran
3d printing: additive manufacturing, rapid prototyping, fdm, sla, sls
cnc: computer numerical control, machining, milling, turning, lathe
gd&t: geometric dimensioning and tolerancing
tolerance analysis: stack-up analysis, dimensional analysis
materials science: metallurgy, composites, polymers, ceramics
thermodynamics: heat transfer, fluid mechanics, thermal analysis
hvac: heating ventilation air conditioning, mechanical systems

# Electrical & Electronics
pcb design: printed circuit board, altium, eagle, kicad, orcad
embedded systems: microcontroller, firmware, rtos, arm, stm32, arduino
plc: programmable logic controller, ladder logic, allen bradley, siemens
scada: supervisory control, industrial automation
signal processing: dsp, digital signal processing, filter design
power systems: electrical distribution, power electronics, inverter
rf engineering: radio frequency, antenna design, wireless
vhdl: fpga, verilog, digital logic design

# Civil & Structural
structural analysis: structural engineering, load analysis, beam design
concrete design: reinforced concrete, prestressed concrete
steel design: structural steel, connection design
geotechnical: soil mechanics, foundation design, site investigation
surveying: land surveying, gps, total station, lidar
project management: construction management, scheduling, gantt
revit: bim, building information modeling, autodesk
primavera: p6, project scheduling, critical path
autocad civil 3d: civil engineering design, grading

# Quality & Process
six sigma: lean six sigma, dmaic, process improvement
lean manufacturing: kaizen, 5s, value stream mapping, continuous improvement
iso 9001: quality management system, qms
spc: statistical process control, control charts
root cause analysis: rca, 5 why, fishbone diagram, ishikawa
dfmea: design failure mode effects analysis, pfmea, fmea
inspection: quality control, qc, dimensional inspection, cmm

# Safety & Compliance
osha: occupational safety, workplace safety, safety compliance
environmental: eia, environmental impact assessment, ehs
risk assessment: hazard analysis, safety analysis, hazop
nec: national electrical code, electrical safety
asme: mechanical engineering standards, pressure vessel
astm: material testing standards
ieee: electrical engineering standards
