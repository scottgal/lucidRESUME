# Sales skill taxonomy
# Format: canonical_term: alias1, alias2, alias3

# Core Sales
b2b sales: business-to-business, enterprise sales, corporate sales
b2c sales: business-to-consumer, retail sales, direct sales
inside sales: phone sales, remote selling, virtual sales
outside sales: field sales, territory management, door-to-door
account management: key account management, kam, client management, customer success
business development: biz dev, new business acquisition, prospecting
lead generation: lead qualification, pipeline building, outbound
cold calling: outbound calling, telemarketing, warm calling
closing: deal closing, contract negotiation, agreement signing
upselling: cross-selling, revenue expansion, account growth
consultative selling: solution selling, value selling, needs-based selling

# Process & Methodology
salesforce: sfdc, crm, customer relationship management, hubspot, pipedrive, zoho
pipeline management: sales pipeline, opportunity management, funnel management
quota attainment: sales targets, revenue goals, kpi
territory management: territory planning, geographic coverage
proposal writing: rfp response, bid management, sow
contract negotiation: pricing negotiation, deal structuring, terms and conditions
forecasting: sales forecasting, revenue prediction, pipeline forecast

# Marketing Adjacent
digital marketing: online marketing, internet marketing, sem, seo
content marketing: thought leadership, blog writing, whitepapers
social selling: linkedin selling, social media outreach
email marketing: drip campaigns, nurture sequences, outreach
trade shows: events, conferences, exhibitions, booth management
product marketing: go-to-market, gtm, product launch, positioning

# Retail
point of sale: pos, cash register, checkout
merchandising: visual merchandising, product display, planogram
inventory management: stock control, warehouse, supply chain
loss prevention: shrinkage, theft prevention, security
customer service: customer experience, cx, client satisfaction, nps
